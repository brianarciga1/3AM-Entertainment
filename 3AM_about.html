About Us

MEET THE CREW

3AM Entertainment was created by Brian Arciga and Azael Fregoso after realizing that most of their ideas for stories, shows and films came while staying awake late at night, often until 3AM. Having been friends and collaborators in film-making since high school, starting a film studio felt like the natural next step. CINEMEN, the first and currently only 3AM Entertainment series, draws inspiration from their college years and the challenges they face while pursuing their dreams making it into the film industry.

BRIAN

Director ∙ Actor ∙ Editor

Brian is one of the co-creators of 3AM Entertainment and has served as the director and main editor for all of our projects to date, including CINEMEN and The Wanderer. While Brian acts in the productions out of necessity, his true passion lies behind the scenes. He has been making movies since he was 12 and show no signs of stopping anytime soon!

AZAEL

Writer ∙ Actor ∙ Editor

Azael is one of the co-creators of 3AM Entertainment ! He is a self-proclaimed renaissance man with a lot of real estate on his track record. He does everything an artistic soul would do from acting to editing, but whose main artistic focus is writing! From episode scripts to screenplays, you’re sure to find a pencil in this playwright’s hand. Be sure to catch him frowning at the camera on set, and also as his character on CINEMEN!
